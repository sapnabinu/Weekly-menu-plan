Roasted Turkey, Egg Pepper Jack Cheese Breakfast Sandwich

GO BACK

Ingredients

  • 3 oz Dietz & Watson™ Oven Classic Roasted Turkey
  • 2 oz Dietz & Watson™ Pepper Jack Cheese
  • Egg Beaters
  • Canned Green Chilies
  • Whole Grain Sandwhich Thins

Directions

  1. 1 Spray pan with nonstick spray and heat over medium heat. Cook vegetables for four minutes and remove from pan.
  2. 2 Pour ½ cup of eggbeaters to pan. Let cook for two minutes and add cooked vegetables.
  3. 3 Remove omelet onto plate. Put turkey on one slice of bread and place pepper jack cheese on top. Place omelet on other slice of bread.
  4. 4 Grill sandwich before serving
