Turkey, Egg White & Avocado Breakfast Wrap

GO BACK

Ingredients

  • 2 oz Dietz & Watson™ Santa Fe Turkey, cubed
  • 2 oz Dietz & Watson™ Gourmet Lite Turkey, cubed
  • 1/4 cup shredded Dietz & Watson™mozzarella cheese
  • 2 scrambled egg whites 
  • 1 tbsp chives
  • 1/2 cup avocado cubes
  • Sun-dried tomato tortilla

Directions

  1. 1 Spray pan with nonstick spray and heat over medium heat. Pour egg whites to pan.
  2. 2   Let cook for two minutes and add cubed turkey and chives.
  3. 3 Remove omelet onto wrap and add shredded mozzarella and cubed avocados.
  4. 4 After rolling up wrap, grill on all sides until heated through and cheese melts.
