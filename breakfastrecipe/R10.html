Maple Glazed Ham & French Toast Sandwich

GO BACK

Ingredients

  • 3 oz Dietz & Watson™ Maple Glazed Ham
  • 2 Strips Dietz & Watson™ Applewood Smoked Bacon
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 oz Dietz & Watson™ NY Cheddar Cheese
  • 2 slices Bread
  • 3/4 cup Milk
  • 1 tbsp Ground Cinnamon
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract

Directions

  1. 1 Beat together one egg, milk, cinnamon, and vanilla extract in a medium bowl.
  2. 2 Heat lightly oiled skillet over medium heat.
  3. 3 Soak both sliced of bread in milk mixture for 20 seconds on each side.
  4. 4 Cook soaked bread in oiled skillet until lightly browned on both sides, and place on plate.
  5. 5 Fry egg over medium heat and place on one slice of bread.
  6. 6 Lightly brown ham and place on top of the fried egg.
  7. 7 Fry bacon strips and place on top of the ham and fried egg.
  8. 8 Top with cheddar cheese and heat until cheese is melted.
