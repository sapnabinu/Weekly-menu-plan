Originals Organic Turkey & Egg Breakfast Sandwich

GO BACK

Ingredients

  • 1 Multigrain Bagel
  • 1 slice Tomato
  • 1/2 Avocado, sliced
  • 1/4 cup Alfalfa Sprouts
  • 4 slices Dietz & Watson Originals Organic Turkey Breast
  • 1 slice Dietz & Watson Originals Yellow Cheddar Cheese
  • 1 Egg

Directions

  1. 1 Toast Bagel
  2. 2 Fry egg in small pan until yolk is cooked over medium
  3. 3 While egg is cooking, toast bagel and slice avocado and tomato
  4. 4 Layer Dietz & Watson Originals Organic Turkey Breast, Dietz & Watson Originals Yellow Cheddar Cheese, avocado and tomato on bottom half of bagel
  5. 5 Top with egg, sprouts and top half of bagel
