Gourmet Bacon & Egg Biscuit

GO BACK

Ingredients

  • 3 Biscuits
  • 3 Egg Whites
  • 3 strips Dietz & Watson™ Bacon
  • 3 oz Baby Spinach
  • 6 tbsp Mustard Egg Salad
  • 3 tbsp Diced Tomatos

Directions

  1. 1 Lightly scramble egg whites in frying pan over medium heat.
  2. 2 Slice biscuits in half and place egg whites on each bottom half of the 3 biscuits.
  3. 3 Fry bacon strips over medium heat.
  4. 4 Place one strip of cooked bacon on top of each egg white sandwich.
  5. 5 Top the bacon and egg whites with baby spinach, diced tomatoes, mustard egg salad, and top with remaining biscuit tops.
