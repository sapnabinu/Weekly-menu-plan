Grilled Sausages

GO BACK

Ingredients

  • 10 Originals Asiago & Spinach Chicken Sausages
  • 10 Bratwurst
  • 6 Smoked Beef Hot Sausages
  • 3 LBS. Fingerling potatoes
  • ¼ CUP Horseradish Mustard
  • 4 TBSP Butter
  • 4 CUPS Chicken Stock
  • 5 CUPS Old Fashioned Barrel Sauerkraut

Directions

  1. 1 Wash and cut potatoes in half. Season with salt, pepper, and olive oil and roast in an oven at 350°F for 12 minutes.
  2. 2 Grill all the Originals Asiago & Spinach Chicken Sausages, Bratwurst, and Smoked Beef Hot Sausages, and cut into bite sized pieces. Hold warm in chicken stock.
  3. 3 Mix potatoes, Horseradish Mustard, butter, and Old Fashioned Barrel Sauerkraut together. Serve hot in a bowl.
