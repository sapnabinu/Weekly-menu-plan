JERK CHICKEN SAUSAGE TACOS

GO BACK

Ingredients

  • 1 Jerk Chicken Sausage
  • 2 Cherry tomatoes, sliced
  • 1/8 cup Black beans
  • 1/2 avocado, diced
  • 1 sprig cilantro
  • 1 tortilla
  • 1 Tbsp Sour Cream

Directions

  1. 1 To cook Jerk Chicken Sausage, cut sausage into 1/2 inch slices and pan sear on medium heat. Cook until golden brown
  2. 2 Place sausage slices in tortilla and top with tomatoes, beans, avocado, and cilantro.
  3. 3 Serve with sour cream.
