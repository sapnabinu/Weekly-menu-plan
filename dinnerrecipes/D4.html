Originals Sriracha Chicken Sausages in a Blanket

Ingredients

  • 6 Sriracha Chicken Sausages, cut into thirds
  • 2 Cans of crescent rolls

Directions

  1. 1 Preheat oven to 375°F.
  2. 2 Unroll both cans of crescent rolls and separate into 16 triangles.
  3. 3 Place Dietz and Watson Sriracha Chicken Sausages piece at thin end of cresecent triangle and roll to the other end.
  4. 4 Place rolled sausages on to ungreased cookie sheet
  5. 5 Bake for 15 minutes
  6. 6 Let cool for 2 minutes thens serve.
