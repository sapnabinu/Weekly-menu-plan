Chicken Sausage and Watermelon Skewers

GO BACK

Ingredients

  • 4 Dietz and Watson Jerk Chicken Sausage cut into 3 pieces
  • 4 cups watermelon, seedless and cut into 1x1” cubes

Directions

  1. 1 Preheat a grill to medium.
  2. 2 Thread chicken sausage piece, followed by a watermelon cube. Continue until skewer is covered.
  3. 3 Place skewers on grill grates and cook, turning until grill marks appear.
