Ham & Pineapple Hawaiian Style Pizza

GO BACK

Ingredients

  • 12 in Pizza Crust (favorite homemade recipe or store bought)
  • 1/2 cup Pizza Sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups Mozzarella Cheese, shredded
  • 1/2 cup Ham, sliced or chopped
  • 1/2 cup Pineapple Chunks (canned, fresh or directly from the D&W Chef Carved Dinner Ham)
  • 3 slices Bacon, cooked and crumbled

Directions

  1. 1 Prepare the pizza dough following direction of recipe or package
  2. 2 Spread sauce evenly on the pizza dough
  3. 3 Top with D&W Mozzarella Cheese
  4. 4 Cube/slice Ham and top pizza
  5. 5 Next, top with pineapple pieces
  6. 6 Sprinkle with Bacon crumbles
  7. 7 Bake for 8-10 minutes at 450 degrees
  8. 8 Slice pizza, let cool and serve immediately
