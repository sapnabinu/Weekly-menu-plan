Bacon Muenster Burger

GO BACK

Ingredients

  • 4 slices Bacon
  • 2 Slices Muenster Cheese
  • 1 Sour Garlic Pickle, chopped
  • 1 hamburger
  • 1 brioche hamburger bun
  • 2 leaves Boston lettuce
  • 2 slices tomato
  • 1 slice red onion

Directions

  1. 1 Preheat grill to medium-high heat, and cook burger to desired temperature.
  2. 2 Grill bacon on medium heat until crispy.
  3. 3 Place lettuce on bottom bun and top with tomato, onion, burger, Munster cheese, bacon, and pickle
